FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Remote Monitoring System For Vehicle performance?

Remote Monitoring System For Vehicle performance is a vehicle monitoring system that provides real-time analytics of your car's sensors and systems through an intuitive dashboard.

How does your system gather data from the vehicle?

Our system uses a hardware-based approach , utilizing various integrated sensors (RPM, Temperature, Humidity, Voltage,Vibration, Weight, Tilt). The raw data is collected and processed by the Microcontroller before being transmitted to the gateway.

What sensors are used?

We support a wide range of sensors including temperature, humidity, vibration, load cells, MPU6050 motion sensors, and more.

How often does the data update?

Data is collected and sent to the cloud very quickly, often achieving an update rate of every few seconds, giving you real-time information.

Do you offer technical support?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 technical support through multiple channels including email, phone.

Do I need constant internet for it to work?

No. The system uses the CAN Bus inside the car, so critical data is always collected even if the Wi-Fi is poor or unavailable.
