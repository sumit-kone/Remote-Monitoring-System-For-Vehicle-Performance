ABOUT Remote Monitoring System For Vehicle Performance

Revolutionizing vehicle monitoring through IoT technology and real-time analytics

OUR MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

To create, design and implement a software & hardware-based system utilizing ESP32, and the CAN Bus protocol that collects, and displays key vehicle performance metrics (RPM, temperature, vibration, etc.) in real-time, minimizing breakdowns, and reducing maintenance costs for vehicle owners.

Our Vision

To be the reliable, cost-effective, and energy-efficient standard for real-time vehicle health monitoring, setting a new benchmark for proactive maintenance and safety in monitoring system.

Our Approach

Combining sensor technology with real-data analytics to deliver actionable insights and maintenance capabilities.
